About Me

Hey! I'm a student at the University of Washington's Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science of Engineering, where I'm currently focused on systems programming and its applications. My personal interests tend to lie at inmost workings of software and where it meets the hardware its running on. As such, most of my personal projects tend to focus on topics related to programming languages, networking, and OS/kernel level programming.
